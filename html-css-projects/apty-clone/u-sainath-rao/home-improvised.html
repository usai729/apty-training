Apty’s Al-driven in-app guidance streamlines and accelerates software adoption, improves onboarding, reduces IT support costs, and boosts ROI with our Digital Adoption Platform.​

Apty is a next-generation Digital Adoption Platform (DAP) designed to simplify complex enterprise software and enhance user productivity. By leveraging AI-driven guidance and real-time workflow optimization, Apty helps organizations boost software utilization and realize ROI. With features like in-app guidance and continuous monitoring, Apty provides targeted help to improve adoption at the user level. Trusted by over 1,000 customers globally, Apty supports applications across various industries, including CRM, ERP, HRMS, and more. The platform aims to streamline digital transformation, learning, and business process compliance. Apty is committed to values like innovation, growth, inclusion, and integrity. It enables enterprises to maximize software efficiency and improve team performance.
